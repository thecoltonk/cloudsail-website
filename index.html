About us.

We push the limits.

Hi! We're The Cloudsail Project (aka Cloudsail), a community-funded 501(c)3 nonprofit built to give cloud tech more meaning. Your donations cover the hardware, software, and engineering costs that keep all of Cloudsail alive. You're actively providing exploit-free game servers, hands-on education, and global internet mapping and digital archival worldwide. Help keep Cloudsail afloat with a donation today!

Count me in!

Why Cloudsail?

Engineered for everyone.

We're engineered for everyone.

We're built to empower everyone. We give creators the ability and resources to focus on what matters: building, learning, and having as much fun as possible. Here's how we help:

Here’s a fact that people recognize but don’t act upon: the internet is a dangerous place. Unfortunately, that extends to public gaming servers that many kids and teens play. At Cloudsail, we build free, safe, and moderated digital servers that are free from the scammy, exploitative practices employed by other servers.

Meet the team.

We get it done.

If it's Cloudsail, it's built by us. Our senior leadership team is responsible for making sure anything involving Cloudsail is perfect. SRE just got serious.

Colton | @Passport5

Owner & Founder, The Cloudsail Project

Owen | @Wowen100

Director of Community & Founding Partner

Riley | @CatfishFr

Director of Server Config & Optimization

Theo | @ARandomDerp

Director of Moderation & Safety

Owen specializes in content creation. His YouTube channel has over 250 subscribers and is quickly growing. His dedication to creating high-quality content makes him the perfect fit for marketing, promotional videos, and posts.

Outside of Cloudsail, Owen loves gaming, professional editiing in Adobe Premiere Pro, and loves theater. He also runs a rapidly growing YouTube channel.


Riley, the Director of Server Config and Optimization, ensures that Cloudsail servers are equipped with the latest and most effective features. His work has created a more inclusive server environment with reduced waste.

Outside of Cloudsail, he is passionate about video editing in DaVinci Resolve and understanding what makes a video perfect. He's not into social media and decided his contact links should be a joke instead.

Theo, our Director of Safety and Moderation, is dedicated to maintaining a safe and welcoming environment for all users on our servers. He's always upbeat and making jokes.

When he's not moderating, Theo enjoys coding and development, particularly interested in understanding backend systems. He's pursuing a career in tech.

Colton is the owner and founder of The Cloudsail Project. He's responsible for server uptime, troubleshooting, engineering, and more.


Beyond Cloudsail, Colton enjoys tackling other networking projects and pursues his passion for engineering.
